features of Meghalya

best places to visit

Shillong

Shillong Peak: Offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding hills.

Elephant Falls: A beautiful, multi-tiered waterfall located near Shillong.

Ward's Lake: A serene man-made lake in the heart of the city, perfect for a leisurely stroll or boating.

Don Bosco Museum: A cultural museum showcasing the rich heritage of the northeastern tribes.

Cherrapunji (Sohra)

Nohkalikai Falls: One of the tallest plunge waterfalls in India, offering breathtaking views.

Mawsmai Cave: A limestone cave with stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations.

Living Root Bridges: Ingenious bridges made from the roots of rubber trees, unique to this region.

Seven Sisters Falls: A series of seven waterfalls cascading down the hills, especially beautiful during the monsoon.

Mawlynnong

Key Attractions: Cleanest Village in Asia, Living Root Bridge, and Sky View.

Why Visit: Mawlynnong is famous for its cleanliness, eco-friendly practices, and serene environment, making it an idyllic spot for experiencing traditional Khasi village life.
